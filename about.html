ABOUT US

The Lord’s Venture is a Company that operates in diverse business sectors within New Jersey. It was established in 2017 with the aim to meet the needs of the people, with 90% of Jersey residents living in urban centers, New Jersey has the highest urban population in the United States. It is the only state where all its counties are classified as metropolitan areas with the densest system of highways and railroads in the US.

The Lord’s Ventures was established to satisfy some of the needs of the highly populated Jersey by creating a diverse business that functions under one body. We first met the needs of the people by matching employers and employees together and by establishing a business center that services the basic needs of the people.

OUR HISTORY

We started as a transportation company in 2017. We started by matching staffing agencies with employees and offering our transport services to employees we supplied to the staffing agencies, not only did our company supply employees, but was involved in buying and selling of cars.

We supplied employees for various staffing agencies across New Jersey. Today we supply employees to over 20 companies through both staffing agencies and directly with over 200 current employees we match with employers. Our agency is always pleased to provide you supplemental logistics staffing. Apart from our staffing solution ( THE LORD STAFFING ), we also pride ourselves at being the foremost business center in Irvington with a high diversity in business operations ranging from official services to domestic and entertainment services.
