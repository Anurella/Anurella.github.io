Grab The Mic Challenge

Grab the mic challenge is an initiative from the stables of BLUESTEAM ENT, the aim is to give upcoming artists a chance to work with the producers of BLUESTEAM ENT. The winner of the challenge gets a full session which includes production, recording, mixing and mastering as well as promotion of one track.
To participate in this competition, download the one minute instrumental below, record over it and send to BLUESTEAM email address:"bsteamentertainment@gmail.com"
Thereafter, the best ten entries will be contacted to make a short video singing their entry song, which will be uploaded on BLUESTEAMâ€™s Instagram page "@blue_steam_music". Once the videos have been uploaded, voting will commence, the entry with the highest vote and two other entries will be selected, qualifying as winner and runner ups.

  • 1st runner up: A free instrumental.
  • 2nd runner up: 5,000 naira
Please note that contestants with illegal votes will be disqualified. We wish all the contestants the best and may the best artist win!!!
